What if my competitors also use your API? ‚ñº
We maintain complete data isolation between clients. If competitors use our system, they're optimizing independently with their own constraints and strategies. The market finds equilibrium naturally. In mature markets with multiple pricewars.api users, pricing stabilizes at rational levels rather than destructive competition.
